About Me

I am former geoscientist and now data analyst. I love learning

Education

I received my B.S. in the Geological Sciences from San Diego State University before going on to spend 3.5 years as a PhD candidate in Stanford's Geological Sciences department.

As a graduate student, I spent most of my time collecting and analyzing data. The data I collected was used to make decisions about how I allocated resources and the directions of my projects.

I also participated in committees where I collaborated with faculty, administrators, and students of multiple departments as a bridge in communication.

Experience
